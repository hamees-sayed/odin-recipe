Tandoori Chicken


A delicious grilled charred spicy chicken.

Tandoori chicken is a South Asian dish of chicken marinated in yogurt and spices and roasted in a tandoor, a cylindrical clay oven.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Tandoori Spice
  • Curd

Recipe Instructions

  1. Mix curd and Tandoori Spice together and marinate chicken in it.
  2. Preheat the Tandoor enough to burn your arm hair.
  3. Once marinated, place the chicken in Tandoor and cook until cooked.
