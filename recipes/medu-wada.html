Medu Wada


A unique and very popular south india dish.

Medu Wada is a South Indian breakfast snack made from Vigna mungo (black lentil). It is usually made in a doughnut shape, with a crispy exterior and soft interior.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Lentils
  • Salt

Recipe Instructions

  1. Blend all the ingredients together and ferment it overnight.
  2. Make ring shape and deep fry in oil
  3. Enjoy with chutney.
