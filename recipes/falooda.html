Falooda


A fresh sweet drink make with milk and ice cream

A falooda is a Mughlai Indian version of a cold dessert made with noodles. It has origins in the Persian dish faloodeh, variants of which are found across West, Central, and South Asia.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Condensed Milk
  • Ice cream (preferably Kulfi)
  • Soft noodles
  • Dry fruits

Recipe Instructions

  1. Reduce the Milk then add condensed milk and chill it.
  2. Put the milk in cup along with noodles and ice cream.
  3. Top it with some dry fruits.
